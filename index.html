KP-01 Classic Dill (16oz) — priced above all mainland brands, below all local Hawaii brands. The only Hawaii-grown traditional dill pickle in the market. Zero direct local competition.
KP-02 Cucumber Kim Chee (12oz) — competing directly with Halm's and A-1, but with a stronger value proposition: 20% more product (12oz vs 10oz) at a lower or equal price, using Keiki cucumbers grown in Waimea.
